METHODOLOGY & ASSUMPTIONS
----------------------------------------
- Repeat customer = placed more than 1 order in the dataset's full date range
  (no rolling time window applied).
- Outliers = IQR method (1.5x IQR) on TotalPrice; Z-score method (>3 sigma) shown
  alongside for comparison, since the two methods can disagree on skewed data.
- RFM scores use quartile-based binning (qcut) on Recency, Frequency, Monetary,
  so thresholds are dataset-relative, not fixed business rules.
- Forecast uses simple linear regression on monthly revenue totals; no
  seasonality component, so caution applies on a low R-squared (see above).
- Profit margins per product are assumed estimates (not actual cost data) and
  should be replaced with real margin figures if available.
